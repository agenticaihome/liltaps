Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support.

What ages is this app for?

Lil Taps is designed for children ages 1-6 playing together with a parent or guardian.

Does this work offline?

Yes! All games work completely offline once downloaded.

How do I exit the app when my child is using it?

The app has a built-in goodbye flow. When the game ends, tap through the celebration, high-five, and sleep screens. The final "locked" screen requires you (the parent) to tap 5 times quickly to exit.
