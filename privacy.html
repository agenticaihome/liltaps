Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Overview

Lil Taps is designed with your family's privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information about you or your child. Ever.

What We Don't Collect

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No photos or videos
  • No location data
  • No gameplay data
  • No analytics or tracking

What Happens on Your Device

All gameplay happens entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. Your subscription status is managed through Apple's App Store, not by us.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's In-App Purchase system for subscriptions. Apple's privacy policy applies to that transaction. We never see your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Lil Taps is designed for children ages 1-6. We take COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) seriously. Since we don't collect any data at all, we're fully compliant.

COPPA Compliance

Lil Taps is designed for children under 13 when used with adult supervision. We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do NOT collect any personal information from children
  • We do NOT use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT allow third-party advertising
  • Parental participation is required for all gameplay
  • No child profile creation or accounts

All in-app purchases are gated by iOS parental controls.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We'll also notify users through an app update.
